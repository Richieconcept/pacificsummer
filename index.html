About

Who We Are

Pacific Summer Travel & Tours Ltd is a fully licensed travel management company (RC: 905463) delivering reliable flight ticketing, e-visa processing, hotel bookings, and travel protocol services for clients across Nigeria and beyond. With a commitment to excellence, transparency, and speed, we ensure every traveler enjoys a seamless and stress-free journey.

  • Flight Ticketing: Local & international airline bookings at the best rates.
  • E-Visa Processing: Professional documentation support and application guidance.
  • Hotel Reservations: Comfortable, affordable accommodation suited to your destination.

With our offices strategically located in Abuja, we provide easy access to reliable travel services for individuals, families, tourists, students, and corporate clients. Our team is dedicated to offering personalized guidance, timely updates, and end-to-end support through every phase of your travel journey.
