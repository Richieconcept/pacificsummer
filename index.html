About

Who We Are

Albajo Travel Agency LTD is a professional travel and tourism company providing reliable visa processing, flight ticketing, hotel reservations, and comprehensive travel support for clients across Nigeria and beyond. We are committed to delivering trusted, transparent, and customer-focused services that make every journey smooth and stress-free.

  • Flight Ticketing: Local and international airline bookings at competitive rates.
  • Visa Processing: Accurate documentation guidance and application support.
  • Hotel Reservations: Comfortable and affordable accommodation worldwide.

Located at No. 12 Zoo Road, Gidan Buhari, Kano State, Albajo Travel Agency LTD serves individuals, families, students, tourists, and corporate travelers. Our experienced team offers personalized assistance, timely updates, and end-to-end support throughout every stage of your travel journey.
